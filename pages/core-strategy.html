CORE STRATEGY

A full exploration of Trading Academy’s proprietary approach to understanding market timing and trading the financial markets. This program builds a solid understanding of the stock market and serves as a strong foundation for expanding into trading leveraged asset classes such as Options, Futures, and Forex.

Pre-requisite Course: Foundational to Trading Academy’s trading curriculum, students learn key industry terminology and concepts like stop-entry-target (SET), order types, watchlist, and trade plans. The course begins with a series of on-demand lessons known as Pre-Essentials to help maximize the classroom experience. The in-class instruction covers Trading Academy’s proprietary trading concepts, including how to identify trading opportunities, Trading Academy’s unique trend analysis technique, utilizing the Decision Matrix, scoring trades using proprietary odds enhancers and properly “sizing” a trading opportunity to minimize risk.

Learning Track (XLT): Key foundational concepts are drilled upon in the XLT, where instructor and students engage in live sessions, during market hours. Trading opportunities are prepared, explained, and explored during each session. Students can apply Core Strategy’s step by step approach to trading that doesn’t rely on complex indicators and conventional technical analysis; but follows a set of rules designed to help identify quality trading opportunities. During XLT sessions, students experience how Core Strategy is applied across multiple timeframes and asset classes. All sessions are recorded for future access.

PROGRAM NAME: CORE STRATEGY

OVERVIEW

In Trading Academy’s Core Strategy Course, students learn the mechanics of how the financial markets work and how the law of supply and demand drives price movement in these markets. This course is designed to teach students how to trade and invest with a similar approach to banks and institutions through skill-building lessons and interactive live-market trading. The Core Strategy offers students a step-by-step process to learn to find trading opportunities with specific targets and a predefined risk management plan, enabling them to trade with skill and confidence. The best part, this approach can be applied to multiple asset classes, including equities (stocks), futures, Forex, and options. Tuition: $7,995

POTENTIAL BENEFITS

Trading Academy’s Core Strategy is designed to offer powerful benefits to those traders and investors who gain the knowledge and master the skill of executing Core Strategy in the financial markets.

  • Purposeful – An objective rule-based strategy with the single purpose of finding appropriate trading and investing opportunities
  • Methodical – Does not rely on multiple complex indicators and conventional technical analysis but follows a sequential set of steps designed to find quality trading and investing opportunities
  • Accessible – The same way one thinks about cutting coupons, shopping for bargains, and understanding the dynamic of wholesale and retail, is the same way to approach trading and investing in the financial markets
  • Adaptable – The same Core Strategy principles used to invest in equity markets are the same principles used to invest in the Futures Market, the Foreign Exchange Market as well as Options trading. Regardless of the timeframe one prefers to trade in, Trading Academy’s Core Strategy can be applied to multiple timeframes, whether one is trading and investing for daily, weekly, monthly or longer-term purposes

COURSE OBJECTIVE

To clearly understand the logic behind Core Strategy. Learn to properly apply Trading Academy’s rule-based strategy to potentially find quality trading and investing opportunities for both the short and long term, which is applicable to multiple asset classes.

DELIVERY AND DURATION

  • On Location in a traditional instructor-led, in-person classroom (7 days: 42 hours)
  • Online in a live virtual classroom that is also recorded (14 sessions, 42 hours)
  • On-Demand video-based lessons (8 hours)
  • Core Strategy Learning Labs extends learning beyond the classroom by providing students with additional practice time in a virtual online environment where specific lab exercises are practiced under the watchful eye of a lab assistant.
  • All delivery modes combine lessons with interactive scenarios, labs, and exercises with access to live financial markets

AUDIENCE

This course is designed for students who are serious about becoming an educated trader or investor.

PRE-REQUISITE

The Online Student Orientation and Core Strategy Pre-Essential videos are recommended pre-requisites for this course. No trading experience is required.

DETAILED COURSE OUTLINE

The following detailed outline contains the topics and activities covered in the XLT-Core Strategy Course:

  • Terminology – Learn the language of trading and investing, as well as the basic terminology of supply and demand

  • Zone-ing – Learn how to apply the 5-Step process for identifying quality zones.
